Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This document explains how we handle your information.

Introduction

Haidakhandi.org is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data. By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide during account creation or profile updates.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with the website, such as pages visited and time spent on the site.
  • Cookies: Data collected through cookies to enhance your browsing experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Personalize your experience on Haidakhandi.org.
  • Send updates, newsletters, and important notifications.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activity.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process.
  • To protect the rights and safety of Haidakhandi.org and its users.
  • With trusted service providers who assist us in website operations, under strict confidentiality agreements.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies allow us to recognize your browser and remember your preferences. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of email communications or marketing messages.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The latest version will always be available on this page. Continued use of Haidakhandi.org constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@haidakhandi.org.
